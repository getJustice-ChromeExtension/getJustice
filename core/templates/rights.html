Know Your Rights



If You Are Arrested

Remain Silent

You have the right to remain silent. The police can use what you say against you. Let them know that you will use your right to remain silent

Stay Calm and Do Not Run

Use your phone to take photos and notes about the stop, but stay calm and do not run.

Ask to speak with an attorney

It is your right. Don’t sign forms you don’t understand or don’t want to sign
